About the Gala

Join us for an elegant evening in black and white. Enjoy cocktails, a 3-course dinner, music, a raffle, a silent auction and more — all supporting lifesaving rescue efforts, veterinary care, and adoptions for kittens, cats, puppies and dogs across Sint Maarten/St Martin.

The Cause

Island Cat Rescue and SXM PAWS each provide spay/neuter servies, medical treatment and community support in addition to rescuing kittens, cats, puppies and dogs. Your generosity keeps animals safe and healthy and helps more pets find a loving home.
